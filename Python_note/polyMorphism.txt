

polyMorphism
------------
Polymorphism means "many forms." It allows one function, method,
 or operator to work in different ways depending on the object.

 What is Operator Overloading?
Operator overloading allows changing the behavior of operators (+, -, *, etc.) for custom objects.

implicit overloading
print(5 + 3)       # Output: 8 (Addition)
print("Hello" + " World")  # Output: Hello World (String Concatenation)
